A PASSAGE THROUGH THE AIRPORT

Next time you’re flying business or first class we can be there to meet you. Not just at the airport, but at your arrival gate. We’ll fast track you through immigration. If you like, we will arrange a chauffeur-driven car to take you from the airport to your destination.


Complimentary Meet & Greet can be availed at
these destinations:
Abu Dhabi, Athens, Bangkok, Barcelona, Beijing,Cape Town, Delhi, Dubai, Istanbul, Johannesburg, Kuala Lumpur, London Heathrow, Madrid, Milan, Mumbai, Paris, Shanghai & Sydney.

 

Meet and Greet is available only on round-trip international flights originating out of India. The number of passengers eligible for complimentary Meet and Greet varies by destination, anywhere from 2-5 people per booking. Complete details will be shared with the Cardmember at the time of booking this service. Additional passengers will be charged a fee and it varies by destination. You are allowed a maximum of two such services per itinerary. Meet & Greet Service is available for a reduced price if booking is in Economy. Reservations must be made a minimum of 96 hours prior to arrival. Cancellation charges may apply if booking is cancelled within 48 hours prior to arrival. Either of Centurion Basic or Centurion Supplementary Cardmembers should be one of the passengers. This benefit is non-transferable. One Complimentary chauffeur-driven car per Meet and Greet service is available in international destinations - Abu Dhabi, Athens, Bangkok, Barcelona, Beijing, Cape Town, Delhi, Dubai, Istanbul, Johannesburg, Kuala Lumpur, London Heathrow, Madrid, Milan, Mumbai, Paris, Shanghai & Sydney, but not in Delhi & Mumbai. Fast-tracking and porter/buggy service is not available in
certain airports.
